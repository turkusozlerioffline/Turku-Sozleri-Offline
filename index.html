English

This Privacy Policy describes how Türkü Sözleri Offline, developed by Efe Gerek (“we”, “our”, or “us”), handles your information.

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users.

The app works completely offline. It does not require an internet connection and does not send or receive any data from remote servers.

3. Third-party Services

No third-party services or analytics tools are integrated that collect user data.

4. Children’s Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
